Comprehensive Heating Services

WEFIX provides complete heating services for residential and commercial properties across London. From boiler installation to central heating repairs, our Gas Safe registered engineers ensure your heating system runs efficiently and safely.

Our Heating Services Include:

  • Boiler installation and replacement
  • Boiler servicing and repairs
  • Central heating installation
  • Radiator installation and repair
  • Power flushing
  • Heating system maintenance
  • Thermostat installation and upgrades
  • Emergency heating repairs
  • Energy-efficient heating solutions
  • Annual boiler safety checks

Gas Safe Registered Engineers

All our heating engineers are Gas Safe registered, ensuring that all gas work is carried out to the highest safety standards. We work with all major boiler brands and can provide expert advice on the most efficient heating solutions for your property.

Don't wait for winter to get your heating system checked. Regular maintenance can prevent costly breakdowns and improve energy efficiency. Contact WEFIX today for a heating system assessment.
