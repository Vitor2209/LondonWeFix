About WEFIX

London WeFix was established to meet the growing demand for a modern, more reliable standard of property services in London. Inspired by the legacy of Stephen Thomas with 20 years of experience—who in 2025 founded what is one of the city’s most recognised and successful independent electricians —London WeFix continues that tradition with a fresh, customer-focused approach and a commitment to exceptional workmanship.

London WeFix delivers a full range of property maintenance solutions across London, covering plumbing, heating, drainage, electrical work, air-conditioning services, roofing repairs, general building projects, and complete bathroom installations.

With experienced specialists available 24 hours a day, we’re prepared to manage any emergency and deliver peace of mind whenever you need us. Plumbing, Electricians, Drainage Specialist Services; High-quality plumbing and many more services for London homes and businesses.

About US

Founded on Experience, Integrity, and Opportunity

London WeFix was founded by Stephen Thomas and Wander Melo, two highly experienced tradesmen dedicated to maintaining the highest standards in building, electrical, and plumbing services across London.

Stephen Thomas, with over 33 years of experience as an electrician, proudly continues his father’s legacy of honesty, diligence, and commitment to quality workmanship. Wander Melo, with 30 years of experience as both an electrician and a plumber, is passionate about creating opportunities for others, just as he once received, enabling aspiring tradespeople to develop their skills and professionalism here in the United Kingdom.

At London WeFix, we are family-oriented, dog lovers, and deeply committed to respecting the environment. We are passionate about nurturing the next generation of skilled tradespeople who will help maintain and enhance the vibrancy of the City of London.

We appreciate every opportunity given to us and are dedicated to building a functional, sustainable, and vibrant city for our sons, daughters, and future generations. We are proud to contribute to the growth of our communities with integrity, passion, and pride.
