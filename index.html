About WEFIX

WEFIX was created in response to customer demand for a new and superior service after Charlie Mullins who in 1979 founded what was to become London's most successful independent plumbing and heating company.

We provide comprehensive services including plumbing, heating, drains, electrics, air conditioning, roofing, building works, and bathroom installations across London.

Our team of qualified professionals is available 24/7 to handle any emergency, ensuring your property is in safe hands.
