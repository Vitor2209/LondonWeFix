Comprehensive Heating Services

Complete Heating Solutions At London WeFix, we offer a full range of heating services for both residential and commercial properties throughout London. Whether you need a new boiler installed or your central heating system repaired, our Gas Safe registered engineers ensure safe, efficient, and long-lasting results.

What We Offer:

  • Boiler installation and upgrades
  • Boiler repairs and routine servicing
  • Central heating system installations
  • Radiator fitting, repairs, and balancing

  • Thermostat fitting and smart control upgrades
  • Emergency callouts for heating failures
  • Energy-efficient heating options
  • Annual boiler safety inspections

Gas Safe Registered Engineers
